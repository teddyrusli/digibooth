Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digibooth?

Digibooth is a free web application that lets you take photobooth style pictures. Additionally, you can create, edit and share memorable photos with us too!

Are my photos stored on the server?

If you're logged in, yes. You can even access the pictures you've taken with us via the History Page. But if you're anonymous, then no, all your pictures are stored locally and will be gone if you refresh the page before downloading them.

How long is one session?

You get to take 4 pictures every session, with 3 seconds for each.

Can I choose the frame?

Yes, after finishing your photo, you can choose and apply a frame layout and colour of your choice.

Can I add stickers?

Yes, definetly! You can add stickers to your result after picking your layout and colours.

How can I contact your team?

Visit the Contact Us Page to send a message directly to barisdua!
