About Us

About "Baris Dua"

We are students from the Computer Science Department, class of 2024, who are currently in our third semester. Who are we, exactly? We call ourselves Baris Dua — haha, sounds a bit funny, but that’s the truth.

Why “Baris Dua”? Its' not an official name, but its cuz when we first met during orientation (MPLS), we were coincidentally all sitting in the second row in class, and even now, we’re still sitting in the second row! We quickly became friends and have worked on multiple team-based assignments together.

Lately, we’ve been learning about how to build a website. For this project assignment, we decided to learn how to create a website inspired by digital photobooths. The reason we chose Digibooth is simply because we love photobooths, even though there are already many similar ones out there in the market.

Our goal is to create meaningful web applications that people can enjoy and interact with easily. Every detail, from layout to functionality, is crafted with care and teamwork, (and maybe some ai assistance xD)

About Digibooth

Digibooth is a simple web-based photo booth app that allows users to take, edit, and share their favorite memories directly from their browser — no app downloads required.

Our mission is to make digital creativity accessible, safe, and fun for everyone.

With Digibooth, you can explore various frames, stickers, and filters to personalize your photos effortlessly. It’s a free, lightweight and modern alternative to actually going to a traditional photobooth.
